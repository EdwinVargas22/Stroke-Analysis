Summary: Stroke in the United States

According to the World Health Organization, 15 million people suffer from a stroke worldwide each year. Of these, 5 million die and another 5 million are permanently disabled in some way.

For our project, we analyzed a subset of these stroke individuals to identify the various factors that may contribute to stroke risk, and use machine learning to identify the risk of stroke.
